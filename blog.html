The Problem

\n\n\n\n

Real estate agencies potentially have over a thousand clients (landlords & tenants) and potentially have tens of thousands of events to keep track of such as payments, lease renewals, etc.

\n\n\n\n

So, how do you communicate with all these people? And how do you keep track of everything?

\n\n\n\n

The Problem Quantified

\n\n\n\n

Let’s quantify this by looking into two scenarios. A medium sized agency with 300 properties under management and a large agency with 1000 properties. We will assume each property has one landlord and one tenant. To work out the amount of payments per week, let’s guestimate and multiply the amount of tenants by 70%.

\n\n\n\n

Example 1: Medium Sized Agency (300 Properties Under Management)

\n\n\n\n

300 Properties = 300 Tenants + 300 Landlords = 600 Clients

\n\n\n\n

300 Tenants * 70% = 210 Payments Per Week
210 Payments PW * 52 Weeks = 10,920 Payments Per Year

\n\n\n\n

Example 2: Large Sized Agency (1000 Properties Under Management)

\n\n\n\n

1000 Properties = 1000 Tenants + 1000 Landlords = 1000 Clients

\n\n\n\n

1000 Tenants * 70% = 700 Payments Per Week
700 Payments PW * 52 Weeks = 36,400 Payments Per Year

\n\n\n\n

It isn’t feasible to send manually send tens of thousands of emails.

\n\n\n\n

A (Simplified) Look At The Tenant Payments Workflow

\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n

What would happen the receipts were not sent to the tenant?

\n\n\n\n

From a customer experience standpoint, the tenant wouldn’t know where their rent is paid up to. And in order to find out, they would have to either email or call the agency. That’s an extra step for the tenants. If it isn’t convenient, people won’t bother.

\n\n\n\n

From the agent’s perspective, there’s added overhead due to the extra phone calls and emails. In addition, managing rental arrears* becomes a bigger pain point. This is something you would want to nip in the bud straight away. Trust me, evicting a tenant takes a lot of time.

\n\n\n\n

\n\n\n\n

* Automated Payments Processing is another potential feature. It isn’t part of the minimum viable product, so it will be out of scope for now.
** Arrears refers to the rent and/or utililies usage owed to the agency.

\n\n\n\n

The Solution

\n\n\n\n

We automate your workflows, so that you can focus on your business.

\n\n\n\n

pmcloud needs to cover all the aspects of property management well and use thoughtful automation to improve workflows. We can use something like MailChimp or Twilio/SendGrid to batch SMS or email our tenants. As for things such as lease renewals, insurance renewals & organising routine inspections, pmcloud needs to provide interactive reports that would allow the property manager to take action.
